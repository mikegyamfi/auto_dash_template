Giving Hope: Our Fundraisers for Orphanages and Lupus Awareness

At Auto Dash, community care is part of our DNA. We proudly organize fundraising initiatives for orphanages to support vulnerable children with food, clothing, and school supplies. Each donation drive is a chance to create brighter futures.

We also take a stand for health by raising awareness. Over the years, we have helped in educating the public about lupus, a disease that affects many but is often misunderstood. Through events, educational materials, and donations, we help raise visibility and hope for those living with lupus. Because helping others is just as important as helping their cars shine.

Health Meets Auto Care: Free Medical and Eye Screenings for the Public

We believe wellness shouldn't stop at the vehicle. That’s why we combine our free car wash event with public health screenings.
